Reprints exist (also in different colors, perforations, papers etc.).


Remainders are often cancelled with "RAMPUR 19 MA 1900 BUSSAHIR STATE" (often with the "1" of "19" missing and the "M" badly done). They exist pasted on small pieces of paper with this cancel.

Some crudely printed forgeries exist without monogram; see http://www.princelystates.com/CurrentIssue/ff-04-01b.shtml for images and more details.


Modern forgeries (1980's?), apparently copied from the Stanley Gibbons catalogue, no monogram. They also exist in bogus colors.
