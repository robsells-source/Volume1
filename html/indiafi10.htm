BIKANER (BIKANIR)


A genuine Bikaner fiscal stamp? Forgeries exist.


Could this be bogus issues for Bhor? Or Courtfee revenues of Bukanir of 1879? The Forbin Guide of fiscal stamps lists them under Bikanir 1884 (the Indian inscription also says Bikaner). The English inscription is very bad "HAF ANA", "QUATR ANA" etc. These are probably the bogus issues that were also offered by the stamp forger Erasmo Oneglia in his 1906 pricelist.


These forgeries were taken from the Fournier Albums in Geneva (thanks to Gerhard Lang for going there and taking pictures of all the forgreries in these albums). They are clearly different from the above stamps.


Inscription "STAMPRAJSRIBIKANIR" Size 52 x 50 mm. 2 a, 4 a, 6 a, 8 a and 12 a, all in red and black. Also a 1 R red and black (size 58 x 51 mm). A 2 R red and black should also exist. These stamps are listed in the Forbin Guide and were issued in 1884 and 1885. All are very rare (and possibly even bogus)
