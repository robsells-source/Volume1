Many states of India issued their own revenue stamps.

 

The Forbin guide lists these stamps upto about 1910.

HINDOL STATE

Court Fee stamps of British India exist with Queen Victoria with overprint "HINDOL STATE" are listed in the Forbin Guide (under Orissa).

HOLKAR/INDORE


Holkar 1886; Court Fees, in this design were issued: 1 a red, 2 a green, 4 a violet and 8 a blue.


This and the next issue with a cancel from Jammu and Kashmir. Not sure what to think of this....... They are all cancelled with the 3-ring cancel of Jammu with date 7 Dec 1891.


Fiscal stamp of Holkar issued in 1890. The values 1 a red, 2 a green, 4 a violet and 8 a blue exist in this design.


1905 issue, 1 a red.


Holkar State, "RECEIPT STAMP"


"INDORE STATE COURT FEE" (reduced sized image). First issued in 1904; I've seen 1 a brown, 2 a red, 4 a blue, 8 a orange, 10 a olive..


Holkar: Stamps very similar to the postage stamps, but with three lines of inscription in the center instead of one line. The values 1 a black on red and 1 a black on yellow were issued in 1885.


Cut from a fiscal document; 2 a blue. In this design I have also seen 8 a olive, 1 R red. Similar documents also exist with portraits of different rulers in the center.

HYDERABAD


Hyderabad fiscal stamp, 1 a black, issued in 1893. Only the 1 a is listed in the Forbin Giuide; but I've also seen 8 a, 1 R, 5 R, 20 R (all in black).

In the Philatelic Record Volume XXXVI July 1914 No 7, page 115, it is stated that a blue fiscal stamp exists with the year '1323' instead of '1283' (the year is indicated in Arabic in the top middle section).


This must be the above mentioned blue fiscal stamp.


Stamp of Berar, overprinted "Hyd. R.B." (Hyderabad Residency Bazar) in red (1892). Also the same overprint on a stamp already overprinted "SECUNDRABAD" or "SECUNDERABAD" (1890)


Red "HYD.R.B." (Hyderabad Residency Bazar) on Foreign Bill stamp of India.


Court fee of 1900; 6 a blue. 4 a red, 4 a green, 6 a grey, 6 a red, 8 a green, 1 R brown should also exist according to the Forbin Guide


"ADHESIVE" 3 R red. I've also seen 4 a black, 8 a green, 1 R green and 5 R red in the same design.


1 a grey "RECEIPT"


Fiscal stamp with "GOVERNMENT OF HYDERABAD RECEIPT" 1 a brown (issued in 1950?). There are two shades of this stamp, a brownish red and a more violet shade.


Forest department stamp of 1924 with two axis and trees at the sides. I've also seen it in red (different value).

JAISALMER (RAJ JAISELMAN)


Large red 'bird' design (62 x 72 mm) and much smaller 'bird' (parrot?) design (28 x 35 mm) together on a fiscal document. Also smaller bird design (enlarged). Large stamp issued befor 1910 since it is listed in Walter Morley's catalogue.

In the Walter Morley catalogue, the large stamp is listed under Raj Jaiselman.


Jaisalmer; Court Fee Stamp with hole cancel. In this design I've seen 1 a red, 2 a violet, 4 a yellow, 8 a green, 1 R blue, 8 R brown. Exists in two types with different letter types; "M" of "STAMP" aligned with inner frame or not.


1928(?) In this design I have seen 1 a red, 2 a lilac, 4 a yellow, 8 a green, 1 R blue, 2 R brown, 4 R green, 8 R brown.


1940: 1 a green, size 37 x 46 mm). Also a red and green stamp in a similar design, but much smaller (19 x 22 mm)
