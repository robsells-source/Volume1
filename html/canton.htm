Fournier forgeries, both the stamp and the overprint are forged. The cancels I have seen on these forgeries are "CANTON 3E/27 FEVR 01 CHINE" (often only partially visible). Note the accent on the "N" of "INDO" in two of the 5 F forgeries.

Forgeries have been made of these stamps and the overprints by Fournier (note that the perforation is imperfect, especially in the corners), for more information on these forgeries click here.


A sheet with forged Fournier overprints as taken from a Fournier Album.

In his 1906 pricelist, the forger Oneglia, offers the values 50 c, 75 c, 1 F and 5 F.
