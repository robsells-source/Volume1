DUSSELDORF

1894 Number, inscription "STADTBRIEF - BEFORDERUNG COURIER", small crosses in the corners, perforated


(Reduced size)

  2 p brown on green
  3 p blue on green
  10 p yellow on green

All these stamps were also used in Kiel and the 3 p blue on green is also used in Munster. Other stamps in different values and colours can also be found in those two cities.

COURIER ERFURT

1896 Inscription "COURIER ERFURT"

  2 p green
  3 p red
  3 p orange


(Postcards with same design)

The above postcard has the inscription: "Brief Beforderung "Courier", Erfurt Karten Brief", an attached part states the cost of sending letters.

 

Some stamps of Leipzig were used in Erfurt, they also exist overprinted with 'EC' and a wheel. There also exist two stamps of the 'Thuringer Volksblatt' in the values 2 p green and 3 p red.
