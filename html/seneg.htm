1887 Stamps of the French colonies of 1881 surcharged:


More types of the '15' surcharge

  '5' (5 types) on 20 c red on green
  '5' (3 types) on 30 c brown
  '10' (4 types) on 4 c brown
  '10' (7 types) on 20 c red on green
  '15' (9 types) on 20 c red on green

The types seem to exist se-tenant. In total, five types exist of the '5' surcharge, seven of the '10' surcharge and nine types of the '15' overprint. Not all types were used on all values.

Forged overprints made by the forger Fournier (as found in the Fournier Album).

The forger Fournier has made forgeries of all types. In the Fournier Album the following forged cancels can be found that he used on his forgeries:
"ST LOUIS A DAKAR 4 JUIN 92 SENEGAL" in a wavy circle
"SENEGAL 2 SEPT 92 ST LOUIS"
"DAKAR 11 JUIL 92 SENEGAL"
"SAINT LOUIS 12 SEPT 92 SENEGAL"
