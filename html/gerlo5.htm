MUNSTER

1897 As stamps of Kiel, inscription "STADTBRIEF BEFORDERUNG COURIER", but with middle left and right ornament changed (arrow removed)

  2 p green
  3 p red


(Similar stamp for Kiel)

The stamps 2 p green and 3 p red of Kiel were also used in Munster. The stamps 2 p brown on green, 3 p blue on green and 10 p yellow on green of Dusseldorf were also used in Munster. All these stamps look alike, their usage in Munster can only be determined by the cancel ("COURIER MUNSTER").

NAUMBURG

NUREMBERG (Nurnberg)

1895 Inscription "COURIER PRIVAT STADTPOST" no city name indicated

  1 1/2 p blue

This stamp is one of the more common local issues of Germany. It has a very strong resemblance to the stamps issued in Munich (central design different though). A postcard 1 1/2 p blue (eagle) exist.
