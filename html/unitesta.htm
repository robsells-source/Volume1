Forgeries exist, examples:


Forgeries of the 3 c and 10 c produced in Germany. Probably by Engelhardt Fohl of Dresden according to the Mitteldeutsche Philatelisten Zeitung (July 1900 issue; see also http://jobi.bizhosting.com/Many%20Faces%20Die%209FG.htm)

There is even a forgery of the 10 c value in the wrong colour blue (see image above for example).

Another forgery of the 6 c has the head of Franklin pointing in the wrong direction (to the right instead of the left). For more forgeries of the 1 c value, see http://www.jobi.bizhosting.com/Many%20Faces%20Die%2012FG.htm. Another forgery of the 1 c exists in which the stars are touching the inner oval.


One forgery of the 1 c has six-rayed stars instead of five.
