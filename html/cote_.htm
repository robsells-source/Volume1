Forgeries:


Fournier forgeries, the second one has the "GRAND-BASSAM */25 MARS 01 COTE D'IVOIRE" cancel that can also be found in the Fournier Album of Philatelic forgeries, however with the year "15" instead of "01".


A block of Fournier forgeries, where the corner perforation imperfections can clearly be observed (the vertical and horizontal perforations do not match in the corners, in the genuine stamps they do).

Fournier has made forgeries of these stamps, for more information on these forgeries click here.
