Fiscal stamps

1854 Crown and inscription "CAPE OF GOOD HOPE"

In this design were issued 1 p brown, 3 p lilac, 6 p blue (2 types, small or large letters in 'SIX PENCE'), 9 p red, 12 p lilac, 3 Sh brown, 10 Sh green, 20 Sh green, 5 Pounds orange and 10 Pounds blue. The size varies according to the value.

 

1861 Crown in ellipse

In this design only a 1/2 p red stamp was issued.

 

1864 Queen Victoria


"THE STAMP ACT 1864"

Queen Victoria, inscription "THE STAMP ACT 1864" all slightly different design. The first stamps were issued in lilac in 1864: 1/2 p, 1 p, 3 p, 6 p, 9 p, 1 Sh, 1 Sh 6 p, 2 Sh, 2 Sh 6 p, 3 Sh, 4 Sh, 4 Sh 6 p, 5 Sh, 6 Sh, 7 Sh, 7 Sh 6 p, 9 Sh, 10 Sh, 12 Sh, 12 Sh 6 p, 15 Sh, 1 Pound, 1.5 Pound 1.10 Pound, 2 Pounds, 2.5 Pounds, 2.10 Pounds, 3 Pounds, 4 Pounds, 5 Pounds, 6 Pounds, 9 Pounds, 10 Pounds, 20 Pounds, 30 Pounds and 50 Pounds. Slightly different colors were issued in 1874 (violet: 1 to 3 p and Pound values; green: 1 Sh to 15 Sh). Again new colors were issued in 1879: 1 p green on yellow, 3 p red on yellow, 6 p brown on yellow, 1 Sh brown, 1 Sh 6 p blue, 2 Sh red, 2 Sh 6 p brown, 3 Sh red, 4 Sh yellow, 4 Sh 6 p green, 5 Sh violet, 6 Sh green, 7 Sh grey, 7 Sh 6 p lilac, 10 Sh violet, 12 Sh orange, 12 Sh 6 p grey, 15 Sh blue, 1 Pound black on yellow, 1 Pound violet on yellow, 1.5 Pound red on yellow, 2 Pound green on yellow, 4 Pounds brown on yellow, 5 Pounds red on green, 10 Pounds black on green. I've seen the values: 1 p green on yellow, 3 p orange on yellow, 6 p brown on yellow, 1 Sh lilac, 2 Sh red, 2 Sh 6 p yellow, 10 Sh lilac, 10 Sh violet and 50 Pounds violet. These stamps overprinted with 'G' were issued for Griqualand.


Other smaller fiscal stamp with image of Queen Victoria, inscription "STAMP DUTY", issued in 1872 in the values 2 p lilac, 2 p green, 4 p lilac, 4 p red, 8 p lilac, 8 Sh green and 8 Sh blue.

("CIGARETTE DUTY" and "CUSTOMS DUTY" on postage stamps of Cape of Good Hope)


"PATENT AND PROPRIETARY" (reduced size)

Some postage stamps of 1903 exist with overprint "CIGARETTE DUTY" (two sizes on 1/2 p green and 1 p red) or 'CUSTOMS DUTY' (1 p red, 2 p brown, 6 p violet and 1 Sh brown).

Also a 1 p red postage stamp of 1903 with "PATENT AND PROPRIETARY".
Larger stamps with inscription "PATENT AND PROPRIETARY" exist (they were issued in 1909): 1 p violet and blue, 2 p violet and red (re-issued in 1910 in slightly different design), 3 p violet and orange, 4 p violet and yellow, 6 p violet and lilac, 1 Sh 6 p violet and blue. The Forbin catalogue also lists a 1 Sh stamp (not issued?).
