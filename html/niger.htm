Fiscal Stamps

Some large-sized fiscal stamps were issued in 1916 with the image of King George V. The following values were issued: 1 p, 3 p, 6 p, 1 Sh (2 colours: green and black/ grey and black), 2 Sh 6 p, 5 Sh, 10 Sh, 1 Pound and 5 Pounds.
