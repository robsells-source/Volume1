1886 Stamps of the French colonies, overprinted:

"Dec 1886 GUY. FRANC."


  '0f05' on 2 c green
  '0f05' on 2 c brown (other serie)

The overprint on the green stamp exists in 3 types, differing in vertical height
1) 12.5 mm with missing "f"
2) 12 mm
3) 10.5 mm
The overprint on the brown stamp only exists in type 1) and 2).
