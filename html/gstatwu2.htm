I have seen a postal stationery of 5 p violet, 5 p green, 15 p violet in the same design. I have also seen a postcard with an impression of a 2 p grey, overprinted with three horizontal bars and a further impression of a 3 p brown just next to this 2 p.

 

These stamps exist with overprint 'Probe zur Frankatur nicht verwendbar' in fancy letters, they were made for educational purposes.


With overprint 'Probe zur Frankatur nicht verwendbar' in fancy letters


Special cancel 'STUTTGART No 1 1819 1919 25 SEPTEMBER W.VERFASSUNGS-TAG'. I've also seen this cancel on the 1919 overprinted with 'Volksstaat Wurttemberg' stamps.
