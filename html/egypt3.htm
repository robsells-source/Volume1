Later, in 1922, these stamps were also overprinted with 'O.H.E.M.S.' and arabic text to serve as official stamps:1 m, 2 m, 3 m, 4 m, 5 m, 10 m blue, 10 m red, 15 m blue (2 types), and 50 m, all with watermark 'Multiple Star and Crescent'.
Furthermore they were overprinted with a crown and arabic text (also in 1922): 1 m, 2 m, 3 m, 4 m, 5 m, 10 m, 15 m (2 types), 20 m, 50 m, 100 m and 200 m, all with watermark 'Multiple Star and Crescent' the 100 m also on watermark 'Star and Crescent'.

I've seen a 'Maryland' forgery of the 100 M on 1 Pound value, the perforation seems to be totally wrong in this stamp (genuine stamps have perforation 13 1/2 x 14).

More stamps in similar designs were issued in 1927.
