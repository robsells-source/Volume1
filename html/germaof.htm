Local 'Regierung' overprints

1923 Wiesbaden 'Regierung' overprint

Three types of 'Regierung' overprints exist for Wiesbaden. Twenty different stamps received this overprint. Most overprints are in black color (except some proofs in green, red or violet colour).

 

1923-24 Frankfurt 'Regierung' overprint

(Sorry, no image available yet, if anybody has an image, please contact me!)

A violet 'Regierung' overprint was applied in Frankfurt on the values 5 Mill., 2 Milld, 5 Milld, 10 Milld, 10 Milld on 20 Mill, 5 p, 10 p and 20 p.
