CALIFORNIA STATE REVENUE

The following values were issued in 1867 (types different for each value): 4 c orange, 8 c brown, 10 c blue, 15 c blue, 20 c brown, 30 c red, 40 c red, 50 c yellow, 50 c brown, 50 c violet, 60 c red, 70 c brown, 70 c orange, 80 c red, 1 $ violet, 1 $ orange, 1 $ yellow, 1.40 $ green, 1.50 $ green, 2 $ violet, 2 $ lilac, 3 $ red, 3 $ blue, 4 $ red, 4 $ green, 5 $ red, 6 $ red, 7 $ blue, 7 $ brown, 8 $ brown, 10 $ brown, 14 $ violet (two shades), 15 $ blue, 15 $ brown, 19 $ brown, 20 $ red, 28 $ violet, 30 $ red, 38 $ violet, 45 $ brown, 45 $ violet, 56 $ violet and $ 56 brown.
All values are imperforate, the values 4 c orange, 10 c blue, 20 c brown, 40 c red, 50 c yellow and 1 $ yellow also exist rouletted.

CALIFORNIA STATE TAX


('EXCHANGE' with red control overprint of the tax controller)

Several circular stamps in the above design were issued. They were first issued in 1856. Several values and designs exist 'BILL OF LADING', 'EXCHANGE' (all with 'FIRST, 'SECOND' etc.), 'INSURANCE' and 'PASSENGER'.
