Forgeries:


Fournier forgeries, note that the perforation does not match in the corners. The cancel appears to be "PORTO NOVO 7 OCT 04 DAHOMEY"


(Fournier forgery, reduced size)

Fournier has made forgeries of these stamps, for more information on these forgeries click here.


Modern forgeries, also with the corner perforation incorrect (as in the Fournier forgeries).
