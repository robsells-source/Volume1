SIKAR (JAIPUR)


Circle 4 AS lilac. In this design I have also seen 1 AS green, 2 AS violet, 4 AS brown


Fiscal stamp of Jaipur with red "SIKAR" overprint.

SIKKIM


Sikkim State, mountain view, 1 a yellow, I've also seen 1 R red. Furthermore I've seen the values 20 p yellow and 1 R blue with 'Revenue' overprint. Also a stamp with "SIKKIM" alone and "Revenue" overprint.

SIRMOOR


"SIRMOOR STATE COURT FEES": 8 as red. Listed in the Forbin Guide and issued in 1880. More court fee stamps exist.


1 a postage stamp with overprint "REVENUE" to become a fiscal stamp.


Postage stamp, here used for revenue purposes (note the star shaped oblitaration hole).

SIROHI


In this design I've seen 1 a red (see above), 2 a lilac, 8 a green, 1 R red, 2 R yellow, 4 R green


1924(?) Two types exist (value inscription slightly different). I've seen 1 a red, 2 a violet, 4 a blue, 8 a green, 1 R blue, 3 R violet, 4 R green, 5 R red, 10 R red, 20 R dark green, 50 R orange (also exists with "RAJASTHAN" overprint).


1945: In this design I have seen: 2 a blue, 4 a brown, 8 a green, 1 R red, 3 R orange, 4 R orange. Also "FOUR ANNAS" and Indian text on 8 a green (see image above).


1944 issue. In this design I have seen 1 a blue, 2 a brown, 4 a violet, 8 a lilac, 1 R blue, 2 R yellow, 3 R orange, 4 R green, 5 R red, 10 R green. Furthermore overprinted "0-2-0" and Indian text = 2 a on 2 R yellow, "0-4-0" and Indian text = 4 a on 3 R orange (see image above), "TWO RUPEES" and Indian text on 3 R orange, "FIVE RUPEES" and Indian text on 4 R green, "TEN RUPEES" and Indian text on 4 R green.


946: The 3 1/2 year old ruler Tej Ram Singhji of Sirohi. I've seen 4 a brown and 8 a green.


1946: The 3 1/2 year old ruler Tej Ram Singhji of Sirohi. I've seen 4 a violet, 8 a lilac


1944: "SIROHI STATE STAMP", temple. I've seen 1 a red on greenish, 2 a red, 4 a red on bluish.


"SIROHI" (manual violet overprint on the left) on Court Fee stamps of King George VI: I've seen: 2 a, 4 a, 1 R Also "SIROHI" in black on the right: 1 R
