No postage stamps were issued in the Virgin Islands of the US. However, some fiscal stamps were issued. They resemble closely fiscal stamps of Danish West Indies, the crown has been replaced by the US eagle, the inscription now reads "INTERNAL REVENUE OF THE US VIRGIN ISLANDS".

I have seen the values 2 c red and green and 10 c green.
