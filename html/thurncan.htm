Typical cancellations

The most common cancellation is the 4 ring cancellation with a number inside (the number being different for every city). Two types of the 4 ring cancels exist to my best knowledge; with large space between the rings and with smaller space between the rings. Three-ring numeral cancels also exist. Furthermore pen cancels and city date cancels can be found.

Beware of forged cancels! Examples:

Forged cancels were applied on the large amount of remainders (many uncancelled stamps are even today quite cheap) to increase their value.


A forged '79' cancel with a 5-rings cancel. There exist no 5-ring cancels (Fünf-Kreisstempel) from Thurn and Taxis!


Forged cancels "MONTABAUR 2 8 1867"
