KALAT


1925(?) 1 a black. I've also seen a 1 a green. The design is very similar to the postage stamps of Las Bela.

KANKER STATE


"KANKER COURT FEES." in a circle (handstamped?).


Court Fee stamp of British India (King George V) with "KANKER STATE" overprint; I've seen the values: 1 a, 4 a, 8 a, 1 R 2 a, 1 R 8 a, 2 R, 3 R, 4 R, 6 R.

Similar Court Fee stamps of British India, but with the portrait of King George VI also exist: I have seen: 1 a, 4 a, 6 a, 12 a, 1 R, 1 R 8 a, 3 R, 5 R.

KATHIAWAR


Kathiawar 1892-1903. Inscription 'KATHIAWAR AGENCY Date... Office of issue... Vendor's signature EIGHT ANNAS Name of Vendee.....". Only this 8 a stamp was issued according to the Forbin guide. But I've also seen 2 a black and 4 a black, 1 R black on brown, 2 R black on red, 3 R black on green(?), 5 R black on brown.


Court Fee stamp of British India, Queen Victoria "Current only in Kathiawar" and Indian text. Also King George V with "CURRENT ONLY IN KATHIAWAR" and Indian text, both horizontally. Always cancelled with a triangular hole?

On Queen Victoria Court Fee stamps I have seen:
"Current only in Kathiawar." and Indian text: 1 a, 4 a, 8 a, 1 R, 2 R, 3 R.

On King Edward VII Court Fee stamps I have seen:
"Current only in Kathiawar" vertically and Indian text: 1 a, 4 a, 8 a, 1 R, 2 R, 3 R, 5 R.

On King George V Court Fee stamps I have seen:
"CURRENT ONLY IN KATHIAWAR" and Indian text both horizontally: 1 a, 4 a, 8 a, 1 R, 2 R.
"Current only in Kathiawar" and Indian text both vertically: 1 a, 4 a, 8 a, 1 R, 2 R, 3 R, 5 R.

With King George V I have seen: 4 a, 6 a, 8 a, 12 a, 1 R 2 a, 1 R 8 a, 2 R, 5 R, 6 R, 10 R, 15 R.


Circular design with inscription "TALBANA STAMP KAWARDHA STATE"; 4 a violet, 8 a violet, 12 a violet.

"KAWARDHA STATE E.S.A. REVENUE"; 1 a brown.

KHAIRAGARH STATE


"KHAIRAGARH STATE REVENUE" on postage stamp of King George V.


King George V Court Fee stamp with "KHAIRAGARH STATE" overprint

I've seen large sized Court Fee stamps of British India with the image of King Edward VII with overprint "KHAIRAGARH STATE" on it in the values: 6 a, 8 a, 1 R 8 a.

I've seen large sized Court Fee stamps of British India with the image of King George V with overprint "KHAIRAGARH STATE" on it in the values: 1 a, 4 a, 6 a, 8 a, 12 a, 1 R, 1 R 2 a, 1 R 8 a, 2 R, 3 R, 4 R, 5 R, 6 R.

I've seen large sized Court Fee stamps of British India with the image of King George VI with overprint "KHAIRAGARH STATE" in two lines: 4 a, 12 a.

KHETRI (JAIPUR)


"KHETRI COURT FEE" (this design also exists as a stamped paper?). Usually a red control number is applied to the bottom half of the stamp. I've seen 1 a, 2 a, 4 a, 8 a, 1 R, 2 R, 5 R (all in violet)


Similar design but a printed design (stamped paper), inscription "KHETRI" alone. Here 4 a violet, I've also seen 1 a, 4 a, 8 a, 1 R, 2 R, 10 R (all in violet). Note the different lettering type in the 2 R shown above.


Very similar to the above design, but with inscription "KHETRI & KOTPULI COURT FEE", here 1 a lilac. I've also seen 8 a violet.


With "STAMP KHETRI." in a violet seal or with Indian text in a black seal, both 1 Rupee. Also a 8 a with two black overprints at the sides.


"JAIPUR STATE REVENUE" with red "KHETRI" overprint.
