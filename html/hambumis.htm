1866 Arms and value of Hamburg, inscription "POST-COUVERT" (April 1866)

  1/2 Schiling black
  1 1/4 Schilling violet
  1 1/2 Schilling red
  2 Schilling red
  3 Schilling blue
  4 Schilling green
  7 Schilling lilac

These envelopes were manufactured by the Prussian State Printing Office in Berlin.


(Whole envelope, note the impressed large watermark in the middle)

I've often seen the following sheets with the values 1/2 s, 1 1/4 s, 2 s, 3 s, 4 s and 7 s in black, are they modern reprints?:

I've also seen many cutouts of the above postal stationary, but in the wrong colors. They could be some kind of modern reprints (I've seen them in the 1980's, but they could be much older). If anyone has more information, please contact me.

Another set of reprints was made by H.L.Koch (1876 onwards) and later by A. Bestelmeyer (1881 onwards). All the above cuts in all colors(!) were reprinted. Information obtained from Paul Ohrt 'Handbuch der Neudrucke'.

Bogus issue

The following label with the inscription "FR. STADT HAMBURG POSTMARKE" 1 Sch, is a bogus issue:

I have also seen the above 'stamp' with cancel: four parallel bars.


Even different types of these labels seem to exist, the first image was obtained thanks to Jamiel Stevens. According to him the 'cancel' appears to be printed on the stamp.


A slightly different type, but with the same cancel.

This bogus issue seems to have been first reported in 1865 (Magazin fur Briefmarken-Sammler, II, No.22, February 1865, page 172).

Fiscal Stamps

1907 Arms of Hamburg, inscription "Stempelmarke Freie und Hansestadt Hamburg"

At least the following values were issued: 5 p orange and yellow, 10 p grey and blue, 10 p olive, 20 p blue (two shades), 25 p brown, 30 p red and lilac, 40 p green (two shades), 50 p violet and grey, 50 p brown and yellow, 60 p red and lilac, 80 p brown and yellow and 50 M brown and blue.
With a violet handstamped overprint "Wert M 0.10" on 80 p(see image above), "Funf Mark" on 5 p.
I have seen the 50 M with surcharge "1 REICHS- MARK" (see image above).


1913 With violet hand overprint "Nur fur Wohnungsnachweis" (housing office tax).

 

1907 Sitting woman, inscription "STEMPELMARKE FREIE UND HANSESTADT HAMBURG"

At least the following values exist: 1 M blue on blue, 1 M grey on grey (1911), 1 1/2 M brown, 2 M brown and yellow, 2 M green on grey (1911), 2 1/2 M dark green and green, 2 1/2 M brown on brown (1911), 3 M orange and yellow, 4 M blue, 5 M red and lilac (see image above), 100 M blue and orange (1920), 200 M red and green (1920), 300 M green (1920).
Furthermore I have seen the following surcharges: "WERT 200 MARK" on 1 M grey on grey (see image above), "WERT 300 MARK" on 2 M green on grey and "WERT 500 MARK" on 3 M orange.

More values exist.
The lower values (5 p to 50 M) seem to exist in two sizes.
With additional text 'Gültig für .... Mark' I have seen: 1 M on 10 p red, 100 M on 50 p orange, 100 M (red) on 5 M lilac (value and text in violet), 400 M (red) on 10 M red (value and text in brown), 500 M on 50 p blue, 500 M (black) on 5 M lilac (value and text in violet), 2000 M (black) on 10 M red (value and text in brown), 500000 M (red) on 20 M green.

1922 New design, inscription "Kai-geld"

I've seen 50 M blue and 100 M red. A 500 M should also exist. Also a "50 Millionen" on 50 M blue was issued in 1923.

 

1923 Pictorial issue, various harbor scenes, "KAIGELD"


1923 Pictorial issue, inscription "KAIVERWALTUNG HAMBURG KAIGELD", here 10000 M red, 1 Million M violet and blue. Various designs from 3000 M to 10 Millionen M.

I've seen
Vertical format: 5000 M green (harbour crane), 10000 M red (harbour), 30000 M blue (ship), 100000 M orange (ship), 500000 M red and blue (arms), 1 Million M blue (design as 5000 M), 5 Millionen M black (design as 10000 M)
Horizontal format (all same design): 10 Millionen M green, 30 Millionen M violet and yellow, 50 Millonen M orange and yellow, 100 Millonen M blue, 500 Millonen M brown and blue (imperforate)

 

1900 Inscription "KAIVERWALTUNG LAGERGELD" (Storage Fees) in an ellipse, Mark values with value repeated twice outside the ellipse

I've seen 10 p redbrown, 50 p green, 2 M grey, 10 M green and red, 20 M blue, 50 M red.


1923 Inflation overprints: "10 Millionen" on 2 M grey and "10 Millionen" on 10 M green and red


1923 "KAIVERWALTUNG LAGERGELD", I've seen: 100 M violet and red, 1000 M blue and red, 50 Tausend M orange and green, 100 Tausend M violet. More values exist.


"QUAI - GELD" from the Hapag company (shield of the company in the center); I've seen 10 Pfennige black, 30 Pfennige blue and 50 Pfennige red.


"KAIBETRIEB H.S.D.G.", Hamburg Sudamerikanische Dampschifffahrts-gesellschaft 5 p red, 5 M blue, 100 Millionen M red, 150 Millonen M blue, 200 millionen M brown and 250 millionen M violet. Likely more values exist.


Another "KAIGELD" 100000 M blue and 300000 M blue stamps from the Hamburg Sudamerikanische Dampschifffahrts-gesellschaft. I've only seen these two values, likely more values exist.
