BAHAWALPUR (BHAWULPORE)


Bahawalpur Court Fee stamps were first issued in 1880 in the values: 1 a red, 2 a red, 4 a orange, 8 a brown, 1 R green, 2 R grey, 5 R blue and 10 R violet; all in different designs. All obliterated stamps I've seen are done so by a applying a hole to the stamp. These stamps were printed by Waterlow & Sons Ltd. Specimens can be found with "WATERLOW & SONS LTD SPECIMEN" printed on it.

Larger Court Fee stamps with the image of the ruler were issued later.

Some receipt stamps (1 a green) were issued in 1875 and later.
