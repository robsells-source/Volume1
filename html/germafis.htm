STEMPEL FUR PRAMIEN - ANLEIHEN (Obligations or Premium Bonds)

1871 Inscription "STEMPEL FUR PRAMIEN - ANLEIHEN GESETZ VOM 8 JUNI 1871"

These stamps exist in two values 5 g (or 17 1/2 k, green and black) and 10 g (or 35 k, orange and black). Both currencies appear on one stamp.

 

FRACHTSTEMPEL (bill of lading)

1899-1923 Bill of lading: "DEUTSCHES REICH FRACHTSTEMPEL", "den" at the bottom (for date)

I have seen in the above design (woman facing the right, first issued in 1899): 5 p brown, 10 p red, 15 p greyish blue, 20 p blue, 25 p orange, 30 p brown, 40 p grey, 50 p lilac and 75 p green. Many color shades exist.
I've seen them with "Danzig" overprints.
These stamps also exist with "Saargebiet" overprint (also manually) in several types. They also exist with overprint in French currency


10 c with "Saargebiet 20 cent." French currency overprint for the Saar region; overprint in three different types

They also exist with "C.G.H.S." overprint for Upper Silesia (1920): I've seen 15 p with a black vertical overprint.

Higher Mark values:

The higher values of this series have a woman facing the left: I have seen: 1 M green and red, 1 1/2 M brown and lilac, 2 M blue and grey, 3 M brown and grey, 4 M blue and grey, 6 M green and lilac, 5 M red (R), 10 M violet an grey (R).
I've also seen the 1 1/2 M brown and lilac and 3 M brown and grey with a red "Danzig" overprint.
These stamps also exist with "Saargebiet" overprint (also manually) in several types. They also exist with overprint in French currency


Here with "1 Fr. Saargebiet" French currency overprint for the Saar region.

They also exist with "C.G.H.S." overprint for Upper Silesia (1920):


With black "C.G.H.S." vertically in black reading up or down (I've also seen 1 M, 1 1/2 M, 2 M, 3 M and 4 M) or "C.G.H.S." in violet horizontally.

STATISTISCHE GEBUHR

1880 STATISTISCHE GEBÜHR, 'Deutsches Zollgebiet' or 'Deutsches Reich':


Statistische Gebuhr; "DEUTSCHES ZOLLGEBIET" and "DEUTSCHES REICH"

Stamps in the above design were first issued in 1880 with inscription "DEUTSCHES ZOLLGEBIET" in the values 5 p blue, 10 p blue, 20 p blue, 50 p blue, 1 M red, 2 M red, 4 M red, 5 M red and 10 M red.
Later (1923) more values were issued with incription "DEUTSCHES REICH", 5 p blue, 10 p blue, 1 M red, 5 M red, 20 M red, 50 M red, 100 M red, 500 M red, 1000 M red, 5000 M red, 10000 M red, 50000 M red, 100000 M red, 500000 M red, 1000000 M blue, 5000000 M blue, 10000000 M blue, 50 Millionen M blue, 100 Millionen blue, 500 Millionen blue, 1 Millarde M blue, 10 Milliarden green, 50 Milliarden green, 100.000.000.000 M (100 Milliarden) green (shown above).
1924, value in Rpf or RM: 2 Rpf orange, 5 Rpf orange, 10 Rpf orange, 50 Rpf orange, 1 RM violet, 5 Rm violet
With inscription "STATISTISCHE ABGABE" (1929), 5 Rpf, 10 Rpf (see image above), 20 Rpf, 40 Rpf, 50 Rpf (all in grey), 1 RM, 5 RM (both in red)

EINKOMMENSTEUER (income tax)

1920 onwards. Income tax: 'Einkommensteuer', various designs were issued, these stamps exist with serpentine perforation

Design resembling the above designs: 10 p red, 50 p blue, 1 M grey, 2 M brown, 5 M green, 10 M green and lilac, 20 M brown, orange and blue, 25 M (rare), 50 M brown and orange (eagle design), 100 M grey and yellow, 200 M brown.

Value in a diamond: 200 M blue and orange, 500 M brown and blue, 1000 M black and lilac, 2000 M red and green, 3000 M violet and light brown, 4000 M green and lilac, 5000 M orange and blue. These stamps exist with serpentine perforation.

Value in a standing ellipse; 6000 M brown and red, 7000 M blue and yellow, 8000 M green, 9000 M violet and grey.

Value in large ellipse: 10000 M red, 20000 M green, 50000 M brown, 100000 M blue, 200000 M brown, 500000 M lilac, 1000000 M orange, 5000000 M red, 10000000 M green, 50000000 M green, 10000000 M light brown, 500000000 M orange, 1 Milliarde green, 5 Milliarden blue, 10 Milliarden lilac, 50 Milliarden brown, 100 Milliarden orange, 500 Milliarden red, 1 Billion olive.

Some of these stamps exist with overprints. I've seen "50 Milliarden Mark" on 50000 M brown:


"Eine Milliarde" on 10 p, "FUNF MILLIARDEN on 50 p, "ZEHN MILLAIRDEN" on 500 M and "EINE BILLION" on 1 M.

STEMPEL ABGABE (stock transfer tax)

1881 Eagle, two different designs


1881 Inscription "REICHS - STEMPEL - ABGABE": 20 p red and black, 1 M red and black and 20 p green and black (1883). These are the only values issued in this design.

 

1880 Stamps in two parts with "REICHS STEMPEL ABGABE" printed in the center of them, value in the center of each half stamp

I've seen (all in brown color with red text): 0,10 M, 0,20 M, 0,30 M, 0,40 M, 0,50 M, 0,60 M, 1,00 M, 2,00 M. More values (up to 30 M) should exist.

 

1888 Same as the 1880 issue, but with the value written out in words on top of each half stamp


1888, perforated in the center, 5 p grey and red. A similar set was issued in 1885.

In this design I have seen:
with grey color and red text: 5 p, 10 p, 20 p, 30 p, 40 p, 50 p, 60 p, 80 p. The value 90 p should also exist;
with olive color and red text: 1 M, 2 M, 3 M, 4 M, 5 M, 6 M, 8 M, 9 M, 10 M, 20 M, 30 M, 50 M, 100 M. The values 7 M, 15 M, 500 M and 1000 M should also exist.

With "W" overprints, these stamps were used as Commodity Future Delivery stamps (Waren Terminhandel, or Operations a terme des Bourses et Marchandises as Forbin calls them), exists with the "W"s printed in the center (1900) or printed at the bottom (1903).
With "L" overprints for lottery tax (two "L"s at the bottom), issued in 1900.


With "W" overprints (Commodity Future Delivery Stamps) and Lottery tax with two "L"s overprinted in black.
