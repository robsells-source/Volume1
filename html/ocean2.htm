1850 A private(?) initiative to promote ocean penny postage:


Designed, engraved & published by J.Valentine, Dundee


Made for Charles Gilpin(?), "THE WORLD AWAITS GREAT BRITAIN'S GREATEST GIFT"

According to the Walter Morley catalogue of 1897, five different designs exist of these ocean penny postage envelopes.


A reprint made by F. Deraedemaeker, below "ONE FRATERNITY" his name is written in this reprint.
