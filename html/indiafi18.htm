KISHENGARH (KISHANGARH)


Fiscal stamps of Kishengarh from 1884?, however they are not listed in the Forbin guide and also not listed in the Walter Morley catalogue. The dimensions are 46 x 43 mm


Kishengarh: Here on a piece of document, together with the printed red seal stationary for revenue purpose.


1884(?) Kishengarh, other design with dimensions 53 x 42 mm.


1899: 2 a yellow (always misspelt "TOW" instead of "TWO"?) and 4 a red. In a slightly larger design were issued: 1 R red, 2 R green 4 R red and 5 R violet. The 2 a and 4 a are 28 x 30 mm and the Rupee values are 32 x 32 mm.

LAS BELA


Las Bela: fiscal stamp of 1899, similar in design to the postage stamps, inscription "LAS BELA STATE RECEIPT STAMP"

Larger sized Court Fee stamps were also issued in Las Bela in 1889; 1 a black on lilac and 8 a black on lilac.

LAKHTAR


1 anna red; only native inscriptions.


8 annas and 1 Rupee.


Local ruler: Thakor Saheb Shri Balbirsinhji Karansinhji, 1 anna red. These stamps exist postally used?

LIMREE


"LIMREE REC & BILL STAMP" with "LS" monogram; issued in 1888 in 1 a green, 1 a blue and in 1 a violet in 1892. The 1 a green and 1 a blue also exist perforated. These are the only stamps listed in the Forbin Guide. Rajkote also issued similar stamps.

 

LOHARU


"LOHARU STATE"; moon and two stars. These stamps were handstamped and cut to shape. The value is added in the four corners.

Large Court Fee stamps also exist for Loharu.
