1862 Queen Victoria, 'adapted desing'

  6 p black

This stamp has perforation 12. The central part of the 'beaded oval' 6 p was used for this stamp and a new outer section was 'adapted'. Some stamps show heavy wear in the central design leaving large portions of the ellipse white (see images above).
