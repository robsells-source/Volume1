RAJASTHAN


Around 1958, provisional issue for Rajasthan, Court Fee stamp of British India with bars and pattern over old value and new value (50 np). Also one with Hindi overprint "Rajasthan Pachas Paisa" (Pachas = 50).


Stamp of Sirohi overprinted "RAJASTHAN".

RAJKOTE


Rajkote, monogram with "R J" in the center. "RAJKOTE REC & BILL STAMP". 1880(?) I presume these are the stamps listed in the Forbin Guide, although it says letters "R S" and the image shown there does not resemble the decscription there. Similar stamps were issued for Limree.

SAKTI
