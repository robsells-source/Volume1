Forgeries of many countries appear with this "FRANKFURT A/M 11 4 74 5-6" cancel:


On a forgery of Egypt


A Newfoundland 6 p Queen Victoria stamp(?) on a Western Australia forgery with the Frankfurt cancel. It seems another forgery of a different country is printed to the left of the first stamp (perhaps Newfoundland 6 p Victoria?)


The Frankfurt cancel on British Guiana, Hawai and St.Vincent (XII c obtained from Gerhard Lang).

This forged(?) cancel resembles an actual German (or North German Confederation?) cancel of 1874, but usually there is extra text in between "FRANKFURT" and the date such as "STADT POST EXP No".
