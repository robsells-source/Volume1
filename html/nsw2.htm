Fiscal stamps

STAMP DUTY

1865 Queen Victoria, inscription 'STAMP DUTY'


(Example, reduced size)

Only this 1 p was issued in the colour lilac. It is actually the lowest value of the next serie.

 

1865 Queen Victoria, inscription 'STAMP DUTY', larger size

In this design I have seen: 4 p blue, 8 p blue, 2 Sh brown, 3 Sh brown, 4 Sh brown, 5 Sh green, 6 Sh green, 9 Sh green and 10 Sh red. It seems that also the values 6 p blue, 1 Sh violet, 2 Sh 6 p brown, 7 Sh green and 8 Sh green exist.

 

1868 Large sized stamp, (so-called 'long-type') inscription 'STAMP DUTY'

In the colours blue and lilac exist the values 4 p, 6 p and 8 p; in the colours brown and lilac the values: 1 Sh , 1 Sh 6 p, 2 Sh, 2 Sh 6 p, 3 Sh and 4 Sh; in the colours green and lilac: 5 Sh, 6 sh, 7 Sh, 8 Sh and 9 Sh; in the colours red and lilac: 10 Sh, 12 Sh 6 p, 15 Sh and 1 Pound.

BEER DUTY


(Beer duty stamp of 1903, reduced size)

Some very larger fiscal stamps with inscription 'BEER DUTY' were issued from 1889 to 1903. They are quite rare, since they were usually destroyed.
