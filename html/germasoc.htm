Insurance Stamps (issued before 1905) for Baden:


Examples, inscription "Dreizehn Wochen II", 2.60 M blue, black and violet, "Dreizehn Wochen III", green, black and violet, "Dreizehn Wochen V", orange, black and violet. This is probably some kind of worker social insurance payment for 13 weeks. I've seen similar stamps (but usually in 1/3 the size as in the last image of 40 p brown and black) for other German States and Germany. For example "Hessen-Nassau", "Elsaﬂ-Lothringen", "Gr.Hessen", "Pfalz", "Posen", "Rheinprovinz", "WOJEW SLASK" or "SLASK" (Silesia).


Here a whole card with these stamps (small and large sized), some from Elsass-Lothringen, some from Pfalz and some from Baden.


Other example, "Hessen-Nassau", inscription "Dreizehn Wochen", 2.60 M blue, black and violet. This is probably some kind of worker social insurance payment for 13 weeks. I've seen similar stamps (but usually in 1/3 the size) for other German States and Germany. For example "Baden", "Berlin", "Braunschweig", "Elsaﬂ-Lothringen", "Gr.Hessen", "Hansestadte", "Pfalz", "Posen", "Rheinprovinz", "Sachsen-Anhalt", "Westfalen".
