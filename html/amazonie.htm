According to 'Les timbres de phantasie' of Georges Chapier (in French), this territory issued (bogus?) stamps around 1895 after a gold-rush. The area was returned to Brazil in 1902? For more information see: http://www.philateria.com/html/story_amazonie.html.


Examples of stamps from Amazonie.

The following values exist: 5 c green, 10 c brown, 50 c orange, 1 F blue and 5 F violet. Some remainders were cancelled to order with red cancels.
