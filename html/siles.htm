In 1920 some stamps of Germany were overprinted with "C.I.H.S" in a small circle


Two certified genuine overprints.

In the Germania type exist with this overprint the values: 2 p grey, 2 1/2 p grey, 3 p brown, 5 p green (red overprint), 7 1/2 p orange, 10 p red, 15 p violet (red overprint), 20 p blue (red overprint), 25 p orange and black on yellow, 30 p orange and black, 35 p brown, 40 p red and black, 50 p violet and black, 60 p brown, 75 p green and black, 80 p red and black on red.
In a larger design exist (Weimar issue): 10 p red, 15 p brown and blue, 25 p green and red, 30 p violet and red.
In a very large design: 1 M red (head post office in Berlin) and 2 M blue (Germany's unity).
