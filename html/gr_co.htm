The shown cancelled stamp (50 c) is probably cancelled to order, since I've seen the whole set cancelled with the same cancel "D'ZAOUDZI MAYOTTE 24 AOUT 05". I have seen the stamps of Mayotte and Anjouan with the same cancel.
