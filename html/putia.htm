Various stamps of India were overprinted with "PATIALA STATE" or "PUTTIALLA STATE" from 1884 onwards.

 

1884 Stamps of India (Queen Victoria) overprinted "PUTTIALLA STATE" in two curves 

  1/2 a green
  1 a brown
  2 a blue
  4 a green
  8 a lilac
  1 R grey

All these stamps have watermark 'Star', except the 4 a which has watermark 'Elephant Head'. The overprint is usually in red, but the 1 a and 8 a exist with red and black overprint:


(Red and black overprint on 8 a)

This overprint was forged by the forger Francois Fournier (even in two types, images can be found in 'The Fournier Album of Philatelic Forgeries'). If anybody has more information, please contact me!
