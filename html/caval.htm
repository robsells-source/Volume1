CAVALLE

1893 Stamps of France overprinted "Cavalle" 

  5 c green (overprint red)
  10 c black (overprint blue)
  15 c blue (overprint red)
  '1 Piastre 1' on 25 c black (overprint blue)
  '2 Piastres 2' on 50 c red (overprint blue)
  '4 Piastres 4' on 1 F green (overprint red)
  '8 Piastres 8' on 2 F brown on blue

Cancels:


A typical cancel is "DEDEAGH TURQUIE" (note the dots at the outside), I'm not sure if the second stamp shown here is genuine or not.

Forged overprints exist. For example the forger Fournier is known to have made forged overprints of "Dedeagh":
