The 40 pa on 1/2 p orange was issued as a provisional issue due to a lack of 40 pa stamps. The overprints were done manually. 6000 stamps were made and they were only valid from 25 February to 1 March 1893. Thousands of reprints were made by a postal employee using the original overprint and cancelling equipment. These reprints show a fully damaged 'S'. See also: http://www.oneps.org/Journal/Art01/BrOff.htm (link no longer valid).
