Postal forgeries

Strassburg forgery (Straﬂburger F‰lschung):

Some very rare postal forgeries were made of the 10 p in 1885 in Strassburg, the so-called 'Straﬂburger F‰lschung'. A picture of a block of 12 of these forgeries can be found at: http://www.museumsstiftung.de. If anybody posesses a better picture, please contact me!

Barmer forgery (Barmer F‰lschung)


Barmer forgeries together with a genuine stamp (most right image); image of uncancelled forgery obtained from http://www.museumsstiftung.de. The left stamps have a 'Langerfeld' cancel. The ornaments at the sides are slightly different.

Towards the end of 1882 a lithographer in Barmen, Mr. Friedrich Wilhelm Riechers forged the 50 p value. 300000 stamps were printed in rows of at most 10 stamps and 140000 stamps were used according to http://www.museumsstiftung.de. This forgery is very deceptive.


I've seen the stamp from the North German Confederation being cut out and presented as a real stamp. I presume the Germany one can also be found like this.
