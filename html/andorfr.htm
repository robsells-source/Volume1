1875 Non issued stamps

12 non issued values with inscription "REPUBLICA ANDORRA" exist (1875) in two types; 5 to 75 cmos (8 values) and 1 pes to 10 pes in an elliptic design. See Cd 2 for images of these stamps.

Later issues:


Inscription "VALLEES D'ANDORRE"


Arms issue, inscription "VALLEES D'ANDORRE"


1938: Postage due stamps in the French postage due design, but with inscription "VALLEES D'ANDORRE". The values 5 c blue, 10 c brown and 2 F lilac also exist. Postage due stamps of France, with overprint "ANDORRE" also exist in the values 5 c blue, 10 c brown, 30 c red, 50 c lilac, 60 c green, 1 F brown, 2 F lilac and 3 F pink (all issued in 1931).
