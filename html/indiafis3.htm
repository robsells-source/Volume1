India fiscal stamps, Court Fees definitive issue and Court Fee Service

Court Fee, provisional issues:

Overprints on postage stamps in 1870:


Bombay issue "COURT FEES" in red on 1 a brown. A 4 a green and 8 a red should also exist with this red overprint


Bombay issue: Service stamps of India with red "COURT FEES" overprint; 1 a brown, 2 a yellow, 4 a green (not shown), 8 a red.


Calcutta issue: black bar through "Serivce' and overprint "Court Fees.": 2 a yellow, 4 a green, 8 a red.


Madras issue, "COURT FEES" in an arc on 2 a yellow. Listen by Forbin, but not by Barefoot.


Many Calcutta issue stamps on an actual Court document.

1870 "COURT FEES." on Small Cause fiscal stamps


1870: Small Cause Court stamp, overprinted "COURT FEE" in red from top to bottom; the values 1 a, 2 a 4 a and 8 a exist. Also Small Cause Court stamps, with "COURT FEE" in red from bottom to top and two black bars covering "SMAL CAUSE" and "COURT"; 13 values were overprinted in this manner ranging from 3 a to 75 R. Usually these stamps are cancelled with a punch hole.

1870 Provisional issue for Bombay. Red vertical overprint "COURT FEES." (either reading upwards or downwards) on Foreign Bill embossed stamps. This overprint exists on the 4 a, 1 R and 4 R stamps.


1870 Calcutta issue: Horizontal ovperint "COURT FEES" overprint and two bars on "FOREIGN BILL"; only the 4 a was issued.

Handwritten "Court Fees" overprint on a "PETITION" stamp..


1867: Foreign bill stamp of 1861, top and bottom cut off, overprinted "PETITION" in green. In 1870 this particular stamp was futher surcharged with a red "COURT FEES" to be used as court fee fiscal.


"SHARE TRANSFER stamps with red "COURT FEES." overprint. The following values exist: 1 a, 2 a, 3 a, 4 a, 8 a, 12 a, 1 R, 5 R, 10 R, and 20 R. The 1 a should also exist overprinted in black(?), but I have never seen it.


Red "COURT FEES." and two red bars on special adhesive stamp. Only this 1 a lilac stamp was overprinted for Court Fees duty.


"Court Fees" on 1 a lilac receipt stamp (Calcutta issue). There also exist a red overprint on the 1/2 a lilac and 1 a lilac stamps (Madras issue).

 

1870 On Telegraph stamps:


Red "COURT FEES" overprint on telegraph stamps; 2 annas (always reading down), 4 a blue and 8 a brown (always reading up)


On Electric Telegraphs stamps, the red "COURT FEES." overprint was applied to 4 a, 1 R and 4 R, issued in 1870. There are even two types of overprint for the 4 R value.
