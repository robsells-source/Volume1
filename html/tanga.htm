Provisional issues of Mafia

Some provisional stamps were issued for the island Mafia by the British. Besides fiscal stamps of German East Africa with overprint "O.H.B.M.S. MAFIA" or "G.R. POST MAFIA", also some stamps of India with overprint "I.E.F." received the overprint "G.R. POST MAFIA".


(Statistik des waarenverkehrs with "O.H.B.M.S. MAFIA" overprint in a circle, I've seen the values 24 pesa red, 12 1/2 heller olive, 25 heller green, 50 heller blue and 1 Rupie violet with this overprint)


(Stamp of India, overprinted "I.E.F" and "G.R. POST MAFIA", image obtained from http://www.sandafayre.com)


(I've been told that these two stamps of India with Mafia overprint are genuine)

A similar large sized stamp with topinscription "KODI" in the value 1 Sh red and lilac is a fiscal stamp (a similar stamp was issued for Kenya):


1 Sh red and lilac. Printed in the so-called Nyasaland key type with the image of King George V and King George VI

One stamp with the image of King George V was issued: 1 Sh red and lilac. A similar stamp 1 Sh red and lilac exists with the image of King George VI. Also with the image of Queen Elizabeth: 1 Sh red and lilac, 5 Sh black and green. All these "KODI" stamps are rare.
