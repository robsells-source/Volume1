A facsimile of the 3 Sh stamp with red overprint "Facsimile". This is actually a forgery distributed with Tobler Swiss Milk Chocolate somewhere in the 1920's or 1930's. It was part of a set of 12 'Old and new postage stamps' facsimiles.
