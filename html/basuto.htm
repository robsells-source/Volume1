Some official stamps with overprint 'OFICIAL' exist of the values 1/2 p, 1 p, 2 p and 6 p. They were issued on 24 Mai 1934 and withdrawn on 25 June 1934. Used stamps are very rare.

The 1 Pound black is actually a fiscal stamp, it has inscription 'REVENUE REVENUE'.
