Specialists distinguish two types for the stamps in the values 3 p to 80 p: small and large head of the 'R' of 'MAERZ' (the 'E' is also different in the middle horizontal stroke). The values 2 p to 25 p have watermark 'horizontal wavy lines', the values 30 p to 80 p 'vertical wavy lines'. All stamps have perforation 11 1/2. Some of these stamps (5 p and 10 p) exist in different values printed side by side or in tete-beche; they come from stamp booklets. Some stamps (5 p and 10 p) also exist with advertisement labels attached to them.
