Express Compagnie Crefeld

1886 Arms and number with wings in ellipse, inscription "EXPRESS COMPAGNIE CREFELD"

  2 p blue
  3 p red

I have seen both values rouletted, the 3 p red also perforated 11 1/2.

Postat stationary in this design exists, I have seen 3 p green with inscription "Crefelder Privat-Post POSTKARTE".

  2 p green
