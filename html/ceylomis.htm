Official stamps

In 1871 stamps with Queen Victoria, overprinted "SERVICE" were prepared. However, they were never put into use.

The values 2p yellow, 6 p brown (red overprint), 8 p brown, 1 Sh violet, 2 Sh blue and 1 p blue and 3 p red of the 1868 were prepared.

The 2 c, 3 c, 5 c and 15 c were forged by the forger Fournier. I presume he applied a forged overprint 'On Service' to genuine stamps. He offers them for 2 Swiss Francs in his Fournier Pricelist of 1914.


Forged overprints made by the forger Fournier, reduced sizes, images obtained from a 'Fournier Album of Philatelic Forgeries'. Among them a forged "On Service" overprint.

I know that the forger Harold Treherne has also made forged "On Service" overprints. I don't know if he forged this issue or the previous issue (or both?).

 

From 1 October 1903 onwards official stamps were no longer used in Ceylon.

"CAVE" and other private overprints
