VINCHOOR COURT (VINCHUR)

The Court Fee stamps of British India of 1872 and 1882 with image of Queen Victoria exist with overprint "Vinchoor Court" or "VINCHUR COURT" (see Forbin Guide or http://www.indiastaterevenues.com/Templates/Vinchur.htm)

1 a green, I've also seen 4 a red and 1 R blue in this design. The earliest use I've seen is 1918. The Forbin Guide says these stamps were issued in 1904. A 8 a should also exist.
