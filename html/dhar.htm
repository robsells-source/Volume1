1897 Native inscriptions

  1/2 p black on red
  1/4 a black on red
  1/2 a black on lilac
  1 a black on green
  2 a black on yellow

Ten types exist of each stamp (as many stamps as there are in one sheet). Most stamps have a black control overprint.

Sheet with 10 types.


The only cancel I've seen is a circular one with "DHAR STATE" and Indian characters in the center in between two lines and a larger Indian character at the bottom of the circle.
