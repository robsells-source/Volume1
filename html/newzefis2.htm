New Zealand Fiscals, part 1

FINE PAID


Stamp duty, but with "FINE PAID" instead of value. Issued in 1868, 3 stamps were issued (different colors)


1880 Stamp duty overprinted "FINE PAID"


Stamp duty, but instead of value, "FINE PAID": 1888

NOT LIABLE


Inscription "NOT LIABLE" (1881)

 

LAW COURTS

These stamps were issued in 1876 and exist in the following values: 1 Sh green, 2 Sh red, 3 Sh blue, 5 Sh red, 6 Sh blue, 10 Sh red, 20 Sh yellow, 1 Pound, 3 Pounds, 5 Pounds and 10 Pounds.

 

LAND AND DEEDS

These stamps were issued in 1877 and exist in the following values: 1 Sh, 2 Sh, 3 Sh, 4 Sh, 5 Sh, 6 Sh, 8 Sh, 10 Sh, 15 Sh, 20 Sh, 3 Pounds, 5 Pounds and 10 Pounds.

 

BEER DUTY

1879 Inscription 'BEER DUTY'


(Beer duty)

About 15 different stamps were issued in the above design. I have seen:
"EIGHTEEN GALLONS", 4 Sh 6p red and blue
"ONE HOGSHEAD", 6 Sh 6 p blue and red.
"TWO GALLONS", 6 p blue and red
"FIVE GALLONS", 7 1/2 p red
"TEN GALLONS", 1 Sh 3 p green and red
"EIGHTEEN GALLONS", 2 Sh 3 p red and blue

 

1886 Larger design

The values 6 p blue, 9 p yellow, 1 Sh 3 p red, 2 Sh 6 p green, 4 Sh 6 p red, 6 Sh 9 p olive, 7 Sh violet, 7 Sh 6 p brown, 9 Sh green and 13 Sh brown exist.

The values 6 p blue, 9 p yellow, 1 Sh 3 p and 6 Sh 9 p olive were reprinted in 1979 on a souvenir sheet containing all four values for the 'Stamp Week 20-26 August'.


1979 sheetlet with reprints. The 6 Sh 9 p is in black (and always has a smudgy part on the word "BEER")


Another bunch of reprints from 1990


Only one stamp was issued with the image of King Edward VII: 26 Sh 6 p red.

 

DISCOUNT STAMP

In 1900 a stamp with inscription 'Discount Stamp' was issued in the colour grey and value 1/4 p.

 

TREASURE


Inscription "N.Z. TREASURE FREE" and crown
