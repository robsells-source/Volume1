GORLITZ (GOERLITZ)

1896 Inscription "COURIER GORLITZ BRIEFBEFOERDERUNG"

  2 p green
  3 p violet
  3 p red
  14 p brown

 

Some parcel stamps with inscription "H.KIENITZ EXPRESS PACKET VERKEHR GOERLITZ" exist:

I have seen the following values: 5 p green, 10 p red, 20 p blue, 30 p brown, 50 p yellow and 100 p lilac. With red overprint 'unfrankirt' I have seen: 5 p green, 10 p red, 20 p blue, 30 p brown, 50 p yellow and 100 p lilac. The cancels on these stamps seem to consists of 5 horizontal violet or black lines with a number in the center. I have seen the numbers "0", "1", "2", "3", and "4".

 

H.Kienitz also used stamps of Breslau (Arnold Freund), with overprint "H.KIENITZ GORLITZ":


(Reduced sizes)

The word 'GORLITZ' was printed on the left hand side for the values 5 p, 10 p, 30 p and 50 p. The values 10 p and 30 p also exist with 'GORLITZ' printed on the right hand side.

GREIZ

Some parcel stamps were issued, inscription "EXPRESS PACKET VERKEHR OSK HOPF GREIZ":

Also some stamps of Leipzig were overprinted 'Greiz'.
