Fiscal stamps

INLAND REVENUE

1898 Queen Victoria

This stamp was issued in 1898. The following values exist: 5 c red, 10 c brown, 25 c blue, 50 c orange, 75 c grey, 1 $ green, 5 $ red, 20 $ brown and 25 $ grey.

1903 King Ewards VII

In this design the following values exist: 25 c blue, 50 c black, 1 $ green, 5 $ lilac, 50 $ violet and 100 $ red.

 

1910 New design

In this design exist the values: 5 c orange, 10 c green, 25 c blue, 50 c black, 1 $ green, 5 $ lilac, 20 $ yellow and 25 $ red.

 

1938 Caribou, inscription 'NEWFOUNDLAND INLAND REVENUE'

The values 5 c red, 10 c black, 25 c green, 50 c blue, 1 $ brown, 2.50 $ yellow, 5 $ green, 20 $ brown, 50 $ orange and 100 $ red exist. A provisional 5 on 25 c green was issued in 1963 (with bar through the old value).

 

OTHER FISCAL STAMPS

A Money Order Tax stamp was issued in 1914 on a 5 c postage stamp with overprint 'Money Order Stamp Tax only.' (two types exits; with and withour stop after 'only'). I don't posess a picture of this stamp (if anybody posesses a picture, please contact me!).

Other fiscal stamps were issued after 1920 and therefore fall outside the scope of this catalogue. For example a Beer Tax stamp was issued in 1938, some Customs Duty stamps in 1925 and 1938 and a Transportation Tax stamp in 1927.
