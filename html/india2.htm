Stamps with overprint "POSTAL SERVICE" are fiscal stamps.

I've seen the 1 a (Indian Postage) and 2 a with this "POSTAL SERVICE" overprint. The values 1/2 a green, 4 a green, 8 a lilac, 1 R red and green and 2 R brown and red also exist.
