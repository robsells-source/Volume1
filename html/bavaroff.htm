1916 Official Stamps, two lions and arms, inscription 'DIENSTMARKE'

  3 pf brown
  5 pf green
  7 1/2 pf green
  10 pf red
  15 pf red
  20 pf blue
  25 pf grey
  30 pf orange
  60 pf green
  1 Mk violet

The watermark on these stamps consists of 'Horizontal wavy lines', the perforation is 11 1/2. Specialists distinguish between greyish paper and white paper for the values 7 1/2 p, 15 p, 20 p and 1 M.
