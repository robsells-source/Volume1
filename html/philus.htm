Fiscal Stamps

GIRO

In 1898 the Giro fiscal stamps of the Spanish Philippines were overprinted "U.S. INTERNAL REVENUE". In 1899 similar stamps were issued, but with inscription "UNITED STATES GIRO PHILIPPINE ISLANDS 1899", example:

In 1900 the year was changed from "1899" to "1900", while in 1901 the year was omitted altogether.

(Reduced sizes)

Internal Revenue stamps were first issued in 1899.
