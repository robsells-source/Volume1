1897: "KGL. PREUSS. EISENBAHNDIRECTION FRANKFURT A.M", 20 p blue, a similar design with "MAINZ" also exists.


1900 "KGL. PREUSS. U. GROSSH. HESS STAATSEISENBAHNEN", 20 p blue and olivebrown. A 80 p red and orange and a 30 p green and light green should also exist.

 


"KGL PREUSS STAATSEISENBAHNEN" (Royal Prussian State Railways, issued 1903?); I have also seen the values 5 p red, 30 p green, 40 p orange, 50 p lilac and 90 p light brown. The 1 p and 2 p are rare.


"PREUSZ. HESS. STAATSEISENBAHNEN", 1897, in 1906(?) issued with black value numbers.


"Privatbahn" overprint for railway companies without their own stamps


,Surcharged in "cts." or "fr" to be used in Saar


and with overprint "Expressgut"


and "C.G.H.S." overprint for Upper Silesia.

1897-1918: Railway stamps with inscription "PREUSZ. HESS. STAATSEISENBAHNEN", I have seen the values: 5 p red, 10 p grey, 20 p orange, 25 p green, 30 p green, 40 p orange, 50 p lilac, 60 p blue, 70 p brown, 80 p yellow, 90 p brown, 1 M grey, 2 M green, 2 M green (value black), 3 M blue (value black), 4 M brown (value black), 5 M yellow (value red), 5 M yellow (value black), 10 M brown and black.
With overprint "Privatbahn" (=private railway). stamps set at the disposal of private railways without their own stamps by the Prussian Railways. I have seen 5 p red, 30 p green, 40 p orange, 50 p lilac, 60 p blue, 90 p olive, 1 M grey, 5 M orange
With slanting overprint 'Expreﬂgut', I've seen 5 p red, 25 p green, 40 p orange, 60 p blue, 70 p brown, 80 p yellow, 90 p brown and 1 M grey.
These stamps also exist with overprint "C.G.H.S." (to be used in Upper Silesia, Commission de Gouvernement Haute Silesie): 5 p red, 10 p grey, 20 p brown, 30 p green, 40 p orange, 50 p lilac, 60 p blue, 70 p brown, 80 p orange, 90 p lightbrown, 1 M grey, 2 M green, 3 M blue, 4 M brown, 5 M orange and 10 M olive.
They also exist with overprint in 'cts.' or 'fr.' to be used in the Saar district. I have seen the values: "20 cts." on 40 c red, "20 cts." on 50 p violet, "20 cts." on 80 c orange, "20 cts." on 1 M grey, "20 cts." on 3 M blue, "20 cts." (red) on 5 M orange and black, "30 cts." on 20 p brown, "30 cts." on 3 M blue, "30 cts." on 4 M brown, "40 cts." (red) on 5 M orange and black, "60 cts." on 3 M blue.


"Rint. - Stadth. Eisenbahn" on Prussian railway stamps.


Later Rinteln- Stadthagener Eisenbahn 1,00 Mark. Also a 1,00 Pfennig error with the comma crossed out


"Konigl. Preuss Militar Eisenbahn"; military railway between Berlin and Juterbog.


The Saxony State Railways issued stamps in a very similar design as the Prussian Railways.
