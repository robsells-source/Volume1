Postage due stamps


A line cancel "St.LOUIS" from Senegal.


Forged cancels "SAINT LOUIS 12 SEPT 92 SENEGAL", "SENEGAL 2 SEPT 92 ST LOUIS", "ST LOUIS A DAKAR 4 JUIN 92 SENEGAL" and "DAKAR 11 JUIL 92 SENEGAL" that can be found in the Fournier Album. The last 1 F is definitively a forgery, the "R" of "PERCEVOIR" is placed too far to the left, the "1" is also placed too far to the left.


A 1 c black postage due forgery of the French Colonies with the same forged "DAKAR 11 JUIL 92 SENEGAL" cancel. See also: French colonies, postage due stamps, primitive forgery type 2. Also a 5 c forgery with forged "ST.LOUIS A DAKAR 4 JUIN 92 SENEGAL" cancel and "SENEGAL 2 SEPT 92 ST LOUIS" that can be found in the Fournier Album.


Fournier forged cancels, the Senegal forged cancels can be found in the 5th row, 2nd, 3rd and 5th cancel.
