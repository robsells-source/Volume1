I've been told that the next very primitive forgeries of mainly British Colonies were made in India, they seem to have been mostly printed on old yellow paper. These might have come from the same source that produced millions of forged Indian States stamps (which I first saw in the late 1980's and which must have been printed by the millions)?


Forgery of Nevis with a "975" cancel and a forgery of Ceylon with the same cancel? Also some forgeries of Jammu and Kashmir with this cancel.


St.Lucia forgeries.

Forgeries of Canada, two of them with the same "C64" cancel.
