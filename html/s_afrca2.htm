Forgeries:


(crude forgery)

There exist rather crude forgeries of the basic stamps (according to Album Weeds, the values 1 p, 4 p, 6 p, 1 Sh and 5 Sh, all without the coloured outline). The "G" of "GOOD" is situated above the head of the woman in these forgeries (in the genuine stamps the first "O" of "GOOD" is situated above the head). This should be an easy test, furthermore these forgeries don't have any watermark. The only cancels on these forgeries that I have seen are some parallel bars (quite widely spaced). This forgery even has been used to make a forged overprint of the 'Four Pence.' on 6 p violet (see image above).


Two other primitive forgeries of the 4 p value. The first one might be a cut from a postcard with 'philatelic images'.


(Sperati forgeries of the 5 Sh orange CA watermark)


Sperati forgery and Sperati forged cancel 'ARI C DE 10 84'

I've been told that the above stamp is a Sperati forgery of the 5 Sh orange stamp with CA watermark. There is a characteristic dot in front of the "C" of "CAPE". The first forgery shown above is cancelled "PORT ELISABETH H DE 12 83 CAPE COLONY" in a squared circle cancel, this cancel was also forged by Sperati. Other cancels that Sperati forged are "PORT ELIZABETH H JY 28 83 CAPE COLONY", "..M's TOWN E E 2 84 ..ONY", "...ABETHT 9 ..ONY" (part of circular cancel), an oval bar cancel and two different numeral "2" cancels. This forgery is not very common.


Sperati forgeries and proof, also black print and the cancels used by Sperati on this forgery.


The Sperati printing plate.

Album Weeds further warns for forged red 'THREE PENCE' on 4 p blue overprints, forged inverted '3' on 3 p overprints and forged 'One Half-penny' on 3 p overprints.

"PATENT AND PROPRIETARY" (reduced size)

The values 1/2 p green and 1 p red exist with overprint "CIGARETTE DUTY", this overprint exists in two sizes and was used for fiscal purposes. On the 1/2 p the overprint is in red colour. Other fiscal overprints are: "CUSTOMS DUTY" on 1 p, 2 p, 3 p (overprint in red), 6 p and 1 Sh and "PATENT AND PROPRIETARY" on 1 p red. Click here for more fiscal stamps of Cape of Good Hope.
