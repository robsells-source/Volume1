DHENKENAL (DHENKANAL)


1890: "DHENKENAL STATE" on Queen Victoria 1 a lilac revenue stamp. I could not find this stamp in the Forbin Guide.


"DHENKANAL C.F. STAMP", two fishes holding a flag, at least the following values exist: "EIGHT ANNAS", "ONE RUPEE". Probably issued in the early 1940's. Also "Four annas", "Eight annas", "One rupee". These also exist with thicker or thinner borders. All of these in black, but I've also seen "ONE ANNA" in green.

DHOLPUR STATE (DHOLEPORE)


1930: "DHOLPUR STATE RECEIPT STAMP": 1 a red, I've also seen 1 a violet in this design.

DHRANGDRHA (DHRANGADHRA)


"DHRANGDHRA STATE" 1 a green, issued somewhere in the 1890's? This stamp is listed in the Forbin Guide, but no date is given. A 1 a red is also described there (no image).


I think this is the 2 annas value of the set (with the value printed in the top right part?). Issued in or before 1903; The following values exist: 2 a, 4 a, 8 a black, 8 a green, 1 R, 2 R, 5 R and 10 R black. It also exists printed with a background pattern. The number of 'loops' at the bottom and top can vary.


Embossed stamp used in 1906, with "DRANGDRA STATE ONE ANNA" and native text.


Issued around 1904.


1945(?) issue

GONDAL


"GONDAL STATE BILL STAMP" from 1881 (the values 1 a blue, 2 a brown, 4 a green, 6 a violet, 6 a red, 8 a yellow, 12 a red, 1 R blue and 2 R red exist). The 1 a blue also exists without the background lines (issued 1891). Also a surcharged stamp "AN 1 AN" on 1 a, 4 a and 8 a exists (1910).


Error with inverted overprint.


And another Gondal revenue (issued 1925?), I've seen 1 a lilac, 2 a blue, 4 a green, 8 a red, 8 a lilac, 1 R lilac, 2 R lilac, 5 R lilac, 20 R lilac. At the bottom of the stamp the number of Annas is indicated with vertical bars and the number of Rupees with circles. I've also seen it with overprint "Saurashtra". Also a 2 annas (in native script) on 1 a lilac.


Shri Sir Bhagvat Sinhji Sagramji; 2 a blue and 2 a violet; I've also seen 2 a violet, 2 a blue, 4 a violet, 8 a red, 8 a violet and 8 a orange.

There are also some fiscal stamps of India, overprinted "GONDAL"; sorry no image available yet.
