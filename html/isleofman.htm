Fiscal stamps were issued on the Isle of Man from 1891 onwards (with image of Queen Victoria).


The following values were issued: 2 p, 4 p , 6 p (all in lilac and black), 1 Sh, 2 Sh, 2 Sh 6 p, 5 Sh, 10 Sh (larger size in green and black color) and 1 Pound, 5 Pounds (lilac and black, even larger size). In 1894/95 the value was printed on the stamps as well in black color.

With the image of King Edward VII I have seen: 1 Sh, 2 Sh, 2 Sh 6 p, 5 Sh (all in green and black). Larger size: 1 Pound lilac and black
With value overprinted: 6 p lilac and black. Larger size: 1 Sh, 2 Sh (all in green and black). Even larger size: 1 Pound lilac and black.


Later issue, here with King George V.

With the image of King George V, value printed on the stamps, I have seen: 2 p, 6 p (all in lilac and black). Larger size: 1 Sh, 2 Sh, 2 Sh 6 p, 5 Sh, 10 Sh (all in green and black). Larger size: 1 Pound lilac and black.

With the image of King George VI, value printed on the stamps, I have seen: 6 p (lilac and black). Larger size: 1 Sh, 2 Sh, 2 Sh 6 p, 5 Sh, 10 Sh (all in green and black). Larger size: 1 Pound lilac and black.

With the image of Queen Elizabeth II, I have seen: 6 p (lilac and black). Larger size: 1 Sh, 2 Sh, 2 Sh 6 p, 5 Sh, 10 Sh (all in green and black). Larger size: 1 Pound, 5 Pounds (all in lilac and black).
With value overprinted on the stamp: 6 p lilac and black. Larger size: 1 Sh, 2 Sh, 2 Sh 6 p (in green and black). Even larger size: 1 Pound lilac and black.
