Other misprints exist, for example with inverted first "T" in "STATE".

These overprints were forged by the forger Francois Fournier. He also forged the cancel 'CHAMBA-STATE CHAMBA 13 MA. 00' with 'CHAMBA STATE' curved above a circular cancel (an image can be found in 'The Fournier Album of Philatelic Forgeries'). If anybody has more information, please contact me!
