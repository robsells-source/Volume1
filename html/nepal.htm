1881 (to 1930) Square stamp with symbol, native inscriptions all around the inner rectangle. 

  1 a blue
  2 a violet
  4 a green

These stamps were issued on white thick paper in 1881 (imperforate and rouletted), on yellowish local paper from 1886 onwards (imperforate and rouletted). The value is indicated in the left bottom and right top vertical texts.
