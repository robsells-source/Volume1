Local fiscal stamps for the city of Reutlingen (Wurttemberg)

Some local fiscal stamps of the city of Reutlingen (Waaggebuhr):

The octogonal design (as the 30 p above) was issued in 1875, the following values exist: 1 p black, 3 p violet, 6 p yellow, 12 p red, 30 p green and 60 p blue.
In the squarish design (as the 50 p) exist: 1 p, 2 p, 3 p, 4 p, 5 p, 6 p, 7 p, 8 p, 9 p (all in black), 10 p brown, 20 p red, 30 p green, 40 p yellow, 50 p red, 60 p blue, 70 p green, 80 p orange, 90 p violet and 1 m black.

Fiscal stamp for Altona


"Polizei-Amt Altona Quittung uber Auskunftsgebuhr"; this stamp was issued in 1906.

Fiscal stamp for Blasewitz


1881; 25 p green, inscription "VISIRMARKE GEMEINDE BLASEWITZ 25 PFENNIGE"

Borna b/Chemnitz


"Ortspolizei Behorde BORNA b/CHEMNITZ"; 25 p blue. Same type as for Hartmannsdorf. This stamp is listed in the Forbin Guide.

Chemnitz


1882 "POLIZEI AMT CHEMNITZ STEMPELMARKE"; 5 M blue and black. The values 2 M orange and black and 3 M red and black should also exist. This stamp is listed in the Forbin Guide.

Deuben


"Visirmarke Gemeinde Deuben"; listed in the Forbin Guide.

Dobeln


Inscription 'VISIRMARKE Stadtrath Dobeln', 25 p green.

Dresden

Arms of Dresden, inscription 'Visirmarke' or 'Visiermarke' and 'K.S. POLIZEI - DIREKTION DRESDEN'


1902 25 p issue

The first stamp in this design was issued in 1863 in the value 2 1/2 n green (imperforate). A perforated version appeared in 1865. The value was changed to 25 pfge green in 1870. A slightly different design (smaller letters) was issued in 1900 (again 25 p green). Finally, in 1902, the inscription was changed from 'Visir' to 'Visier' (25 p green).


1905 issue, inscription 'Konigl. Polizei Direktion Dresden'

In 1905 the above stamp in a new design was issued '25 Pfennig' (in black) and green.

In 1911 a larger sized stamp was issued 50 p green and black.

Gottleuba


"VISIRMARKE", with eagle: 25 p green. Listed in the Forbin Guide.

Lobtau-Dresden


"Polizei-Verwaltung Lobtau-Dresden" 25 p green (1881)

Some 'Visirmarken' with "Gemeinde Lobtau" were issued in 1885:


Yet another issue with "VISIRMARKE POLIZEI-VERWALTUNG LOBTAU-DRESDEN"

Hartmannsdorf


"Ortspolizei-Behorde HARTMANNSDORF"; same type as for Borna bei Chemnitz.

Lobtau


1881 "Polizei-Verwaltung Lobtau-Dresden" 25 p green, listed in the Forbin Guide


1885 "Gemeinde Amt Lobtau" 25 p red. Two different types (inner corners are different). Listed in the Forbin Guide.

Lossnitz


Issued first in 1904 on a white background (25 pfg) and on a red background (30 pfg) and then again a 25 pfg value on a red background in 1909.

Löthain


"Gemeinde Lothain", 25 p green and black, listed in the Forbin Guide

Marienberg


1909 Inscription "VISIRMARKE STADT MARIENBERG" 25 p blue.

Meissen

In 1895 the first fiscal stamps were issued with inscription "Rath zu Meissen" in 25 p green and "Visirmarke RATH ZU MEISSEN" in 30 p green, 50 p green and 1 M green (perforated or imperforate).
A new design with "Visiermarke STADTRAT ZU MEISSEN" appeared in 1905 in the values 25 p green (2 types?), 30 p red, 50 p blue and 1 M yellow.

Fiscal stamp for Niederlossnitz


"VISIRMARKE GEMEINDE NIEDERLOSSNITZ 25 PFENNIGE", two different types (note the top of the "5" or the different lettertype)

Pieschen


"VISIRMARKE GEMEINDE PIESSCHEN" 25 pfennige blue. A 25 p green should also exist.

Fiscal stamp for Plauen i.V.


25 Pfge blue (issued 1905)

Fiscal stamp for Schneeberg


Inscription "STADTRATH SCHNEEBERG STEMPELMARKE"; the values 25 p blue and black, 1 M grey and black and 5 M red and black were issued in 1890. A surcharged stamp "25" on 1 M grey and black was issued in 1905.

Fiscal stamp for Wehlen


1908 inscription "VISIRMARKE POLIZEIVERWALTUNG DER STADT WEHLEN"

Fiscal stamp for Weisser Hirsch


Inscription "VISIRMARKE WEISSER HIRSCH 25 PFENNIGE" 25 p green.

Zittau


"Visirmarke STADRATH ZITTAU Gebuhr 25 Pfg"; a few designs exist (issued from 1869 to 1903).
