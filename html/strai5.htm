A postal forgery of the 50 c (left and middle), together with a genuine 50 c (right). The forgery was made by colouring a genuine1 c black with green dye and repainting the figure of value (thus replacing '1 c' by '50 c'). This forgery can also be found in the book 'Postal Forgeries of the World' by H.G. Leslie Fletcher.
