Wei Hai Wei (China) issued some fiscal stamps (under British influence). These stamps were issued in the Great Britain key fiscal type: :


"ONE CENT" on 1 p red and "TEN CENTS" on 3 p. Also seen a 2 c on 2 p green stamp in a similar design.

The values 50 c on 1 Sh green and 1 $ (red) on 1 Sh green also exist (larger sizes).

 

Note that local stamps also exist for Wei Hai Wei (see volume 2 for more details).
